## Overview
Culture Capture is a boutique AI laboratory with the soul of a high-end fashion editorial. The platform sits on a warm cream canvas rather than the white-or-black default of typical AI tools. Design decisions are made to ensure photography reads as editorial and AI telemetry reads as a printed pull-quote.

## Colors
- **Canvas:** `#f9f7f3` (Warm Cream) — The default page background.
- **Primary:** `#4B3621` (Espresso) — Used for primary typography, structural borders, and hero text.
- **Accent:** `#F43F5E` (Cherry Rose) — Reserved for primary CTAs and status highlights.
- **Ink:** `#202020` (Deep Charcoal) — Primary text color, warmer than pure black to match the cream canvas.
- **Surface Dark:** `#202020` — Used for the "Neural Bridge" code wells and AI logic sections.

## Typography
- **Display (Hero):** Playfair Display. Size: 72px–96px. Weight: 700. LineHeight: 1.0. Letter-spacing: -2px.
- **UI/Body:** Basier Square (or Inter). Size: 14px–18px.
- **Telemetry:** JetBrains Mono. Size: 10px. Used for all HUD logs, vector math, and command lines.

## Shapes & Elevation
- **Interactive Surfaces:** Every button, input, and status badge must be `{rounded.full}` (9999px).
- **Content Cards:** The 3D card uses `{rounded.md}` (10px) to simulate premium physical cardstock.
- **Elevation:** No drop shadows on cream surfaces; elevation is achieved through color-blocking. Shadows are reserved for lifting the 3D card off the canvas.

## Key Characteristics
- **Editorial Spacing:** Generative whitespace at 96px vertical sections to let the typography breathe.
- **Boutique Reveal:** The 3D card transition must feel tactile, with the Typography serving as the "load-bearing decoration".
